Our Mission

Embracing Change through Hispanic/Latino Opportunities (ECHO) is a UW Madison School of Business-affiliated club that aims to empower our local Latino community to achieve success in career paths such as Finance, Accounting, Economics, and Marketing. Through a curated curriculum and opportunities in pro-bono consulting services with Latino-owned startups, ECHO is a community that helps underrepresented professionals shine. Aimed at Hispanic Sophomores and Freshmen UW students in Business who are willing to work toward available board positions, ECHO hopes to foster well-rounded leaders.
